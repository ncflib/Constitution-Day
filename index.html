How would you change the Constitution?

Starting in 2018, the Cook Library at the New College of Florida began asking the local community this question. In 2019, we decided to exhibit responses on an enduring and public website. We will continue to update this website with additional responses after each Constitution Day exhibit (see below). This is a participatory project that reflects the range in opinions about the United States' founding document. Welcome, and please explore.
